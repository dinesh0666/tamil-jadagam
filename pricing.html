FAQ

Is Premium a subscription?
No. ₹99 is a one-time payment that unlocks all premium features for 1 year on that device. No auto-renewal.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major UPI apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit & Credit cards, and Net Banking via Razorpay.
Can I use Premium on multiple devices?
Premium is stored in your browser's local storage. You can unlock on any device, but each device needs its own unlock payment.
How do I generate my chart?
Go to the homepage, enter your name, birth date, time, and city — your chart is ready instantly. Then unlock Premium to see all features.
