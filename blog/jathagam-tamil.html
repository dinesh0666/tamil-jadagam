Jathagam in Tamil — ஜாதகம்

Jathagam (Tamil: ஜாதகம்) is your complete Vedic birth chart, showing the positions of all 9 planets — Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, and Ketu — at the exact moment of your birth. Tamil astrology (Jyotish) has been practised for over 5,000 years and uses the sidereal zodiac with the Lahiri ayanamsa correction. Your Jathagam reveals personality traits, career prospects, marriage compatibility, and the timing of life events through planetary periods called dashas.

Frequently Asked Questions — Jathagam in Tamil

What is Jathagam in Tamil?
Jathagam (ஜாதகம்) is the Tamil word for a Vedic birth chart. It maps all 9 planets at the exact moment of birth and is used to analyse personality, life events, and planetary periods (dasha).
How to get free Jathagam in Tamil online?

What is Rasi chart in Tamil astrology?
The Rasi chart (ராசி சட்டம்) shows each planet's position in one of 12 rasis. South Indian style uses a fixed square grid where Mesha (Aries) is always in the top row.
What is Nakshatra in Tamil Jathagam?
Nakshatra is the birth star — the 27-star lunar mansion the Moon occupied at birth. It determines the start of the Vimshottari Dasha sequence.
