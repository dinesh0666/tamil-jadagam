The Tamil Calendar 2026 (தமிழ் காலண்டர் 2026) covers the Tamil Year Dundubhi (துந்துபி) which runs from April 2026 to March 2027. The Tamil solar calendar (சூரிய சித்தாந்தம்) is based on the Sun's transit through the 12 zodiac signs (Rasis), making each Tamil month begin when the Sun enters a new Rasi — slightly different each year.

Daily Tamil Calendar Features

Our live Tamil Panchangam provides the complete daily almanac including:

  • Nakshatra (நட்சத்திரம்) — the birth star / lunar mansion for the day
  • Tithi (திதி) — lunar day (Prathama to Pournami or Amavasai)
  • Yogam (யோகம்) — one of 27 solar-lunar combinations
  • Karanam (கரணம்) — half-tithi segment
  • Vaaram (வாரம்) — day of the week with planetary lord
  • Rahu Kalam (இராகு காலம்) — inauspicious 90-minute window
  • Nalla Neram (நல்ல நேரம்) — auspicious time for new beginnings
  • Yamagandam / Kuligai — additional inauspicious periods
  • Tamil Date — day within the current Tamil solar month
  • Pournami / Amavasai — full moon and new moon indicators

How to Read a Tamil Calendar

A traditional Tamil calendar (பஞ்சாங்கம்) has five key elements (the "Pancha Angas" or five limbs): Tithi, Vaaram, Nakshatra, Yogam, and Karanam. Each day's almanac is read using these five values together to determine auspiciousness for activities like travel, weddings, business, and prayers.

The Tamil calendar also includes the Tamil year name (one of 60 in the cycle), Tamil month, and Tamil date — all calculated from the Sun's sidereal position using the Lahiri ayanamsa.

Tamil Calendar vs Gregorian Calendar

The Tamil calendar is luni-solar — months are defined by the Sun's transit (solar), while festivals and tithis follow the Moon. This is why Tamil months start on different Gregorian dates each year (unlike the Western calendar which is purely solar). The Tamil New Year always falls around April 13–14 when the Sun enters Mesha (Aries).

FAQ — Tamil Calendar 2026

What Tamil year is 2026?
2026 (Apr–Dec) is Tamil year Dundubhi (துந்துபி), the 57th year of the 60-year cycle. From April 2026 to April 2027.
When is Tamil New Year 2026?
Tamil New Year 2026 (Puthandu) falls on April 14, 2026 — Chithirai 1, Dundubhi year.
How many months in Tamil calendar?
12 months — Chithirai, Vaikasi, Aani, Aadi, Aavani, Purattasi, Aippasi, Karthigai, Margazhi, Thai, Maasi, Panguni.
Is the Tamil calendar the same as the Hindu calendar?
The Tamil calendar is a regional version of the Vedic Hindu calendar. It uses the same Panchangam system but has Tamil-language names and Tamil solar month boundaries specific to South India.
