Telugu Jatakam (జాతకం) is a Vedic birth chart in the Telugu tradition. Also called Janma Patrika (జన్మ పత్రిక), it shows the positions of all 9 planets (Navagrahas) at the exact time of birth. Telugu astrology follows the same Lahiri ayanamsa as Jyotish, with planet and rasi names given in classic Telugu script.

What is birth chart in Telugu?
In Telugu, a birth chart is called Jatakam (జాతకం) or Janma Patrika. It shows planetary positions at birth and is used for predictions, marriage matching, and life analysis.

What is Navamsa in Telugu astrology?
Navamsa (నవాంశ, D9) is the 9th divisional chart. It is the most important divisional chart and is crucial for marriage analysis.
