What is a Kundali / Jathagam?

A Kundali (कुंडली) — called Jathagam (ஜாதகம்) in Tamil and Janma Patrika in Telugu — is your Vedic birth chart. It maps the exact positions of the 9 planets (Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu) at the moment of your birth, in the 12 houses of the zodiac.

Your Kundali is the foundation of Vedic astrology — used to understand personality, career, relationships, health, and the timing of life events through planetary periods (Dashas).
