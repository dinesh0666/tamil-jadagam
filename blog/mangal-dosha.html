What is Mangal Dosha?

Mangal Dosha (also called Chevvai Dosham in Tamil, or Manglik Dosha) is a placement of Mars (Mangal / Chevvai / செவ்வாய்) in certain sensitive houses of the birth chart. Traditionally, this is considered to create challenges in marital harmony, particularly if only one partner has it.

Approximately 50% of people have some form of Mangal Dosha — making it one of the most common topics in Vedic astrology consultations.

Which Houses Cause Mangal Dosha?

1st House (Lagna)
Mars in the ascendant — strong physical nature, aggressive temperament, potential marital friction
2nd House
Mars in the house of family — harsh speech, financial disputes, family conflicts
4th House
Mars in home — domestic unrest, property disputes, challenges with mother
7th House
Mars directly aspects the marriage house — most significant for marital compatibility
8th House
Mars in the house of longevity — traditionally the most severe placement
12th House
Mars in the house of bed pleasures — physical incompatibility, foreign separation

Mangal Dosha Cancellation — When Does It Not Apply?

Mangal Dosha is cancelled (nil) in several conditions — many people who think they are Manglik actually have their dosha cancelled:

✅ Double Manglik
If both the bride and groom have Mangal Dosha, the doshas cancel each other. This is called "Dosha Pariharam".
✅ Mars in Own Sign
If Mars is in Aries (Mesha) or Scorpio (Vrischika) — its own Rasis — the dosha is greatly reduced.
✅ Mars Exalted
Mars in Capricorn (Makara) is exalted — the dosha is significantly weakened.
✅ Jupiter Aspects Mars
If Jupiter aspects Mars or the Dosha house, it neutralises the negative effects.
✅ Moon or Venus with Mars
Benefic conjunctions reduce the severity of the dosha.
✅ After Age 28
Many traditional astrologers hold that Mangal Dosha loses severity after age 28, as Mars matures.

Mangal Dosha Remedies

  • Recite Mangala Chandika Stotram or Hanuman Chalisa on Tuesdays
  • Visit Hanuman or Murugan temples on Tuesdays — offer red flowers
  • Wear a Red Coral (Moonga) gemstone after consulting an astrologer
  • Perform Kuja Dosha Pooja (Mangal Shanti puja) at a temple
  • Fast on Tuesdays (Mangalvar Vrat) — take sattvic food
  • Donate red lentils (masoor dal) or red cloth on Tuesdays
  • Recite Chevvai Stotram (108 names of Mars)

FAQ — Mangal Dosha

Is Mangal Dosha dangerous for marriage?
Mangal Dosha is a traditional consideration, not a certainty. With matching (both partners Manglik) or cancellation conditions, it poses no significant challenge. Millions of happy marriages exist with Mangal Dosha.
How common is Mangal Dosha?
Approximately 40–50% of people have Mars in one of the six Mangal Dosha houses. Among those, many have cancellation conditions — making the "effective" Mangal Dosha rate much lower.
Can a Manglik marry a non-Manglik?
Yes — if the non-Manglik partner has Jupiter or Venus strongly placed in their 7th house, or if cancellation conditions apply. A full Kundali analysis is recommended.
