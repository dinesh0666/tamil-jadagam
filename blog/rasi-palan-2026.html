Your Rasi (ராசி) is your Moon sign — the zodiac sign the Moon was in at your birth. In Tamil astrology, the Moon sign (Rasi) governs your mind, emotions, and daily life more than the Sun sign used in Western astrology. Rasi Palan (ராசிபலன்) is the prediction based on how planetary transits affect your Moon sign.

How to Find Your Rasi

Daily Rasi Palan vs Annual Rasi Palan

The annual Rasi Palan (above) gives a broad overview based on Jupiter and Saturn's year-long transits. For daily Rasi Palan, the Moon's daily transit through each Rasi creates the Chandrabala — the daily strength calculation shown in our Tamil Panchangam.
