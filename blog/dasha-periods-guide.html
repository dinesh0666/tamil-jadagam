The Vimshottari Dasha system is the most widely used planetary period system in Vedic astrology. The word "Vimshottari" means 120 — representing a complete 120-year cycle of 9 planetary periods (Mahadashas). Your birth Nakshatra (the Moon's position at birth) determines which Dasha you begin life in and how much of that period remains at birth.

The 9 Mahadasha Periods

☀
Sun Dasha (Surya) — 6 years
A period of authority, recognition, and father-related events. Career achievements, government dealings, and leadership opportunities peak. Health of the eyes and heart needs attention.
☽
Moon Dasha (Chandra) — 10 years
Emotional sensitivity, mother-related events, travel, and public popularity. Mind and intuition heightened. Good for creative fields, real estate, and nurturing relationships.
♂
Mars Dasha (Mangal) — 7 years
Energy, ambition, siblings, and property matters dominate. Competitive drive is strong — excellent for sports, military, surgery, engineering. Anger management is key.
☊
Rahu Dasha — 18 years
The longest and most transformative Dasha. Rahu amplifies desires, foreign connections, and unconventional paths. Can bring sudden gains or disruptions. Illusions and obsessions need grounding.
♃
Jupiter Dasha (Guru) — 16 years
The most auspicious Mahadasha. Wisdom, children, wealth, spirituality, and higher education flourish. Legal matters resolve favourably. Marriage and expansion are common themes.
♄
Saturn Dasha (Shani) — 19 years
The longest single-planet Dasha. Saturn brings discipline, hard work, delays, and eventual rewards. Karma ripens. Service, industry, and long-term building are favoured. Health of bones and joints needs attention.
☿
Mercury Dasha (Budha) — 17 years
Intellect, communication, business, and education peak. Excellent for writers, traders, IT professionals, and students. Multiple pursuits and quick decisions characterise this period.
☋
Ketu Dasha — 7 years
Spiritual awakening, detachment, and sudden changes. Past-life karmas surface. Occult interests, foreign travel, and moksha-seeking tendencies arise. Can feel isolating but deeply transformative.
♀
Venus Dasha (Shukra) — 20 years
The longest and most pleasurable Dasha for many. Romance, luxury, arts, creativity, and material comforts flourish. Marriage and business partnerships often form during this period.

Antardasha — Sub-Periods Within Each Dasha

Each Mahadasha is divided into 9 sub-periods called Antardasha (Bhukti), each ruled by a different planet. The Antardasha sequence always starts with the Mahadasha lord itself and follows the same order. For example, during Jupiter Dasha, the first sub-period is Jupiter-Jupiter, followed by Jupiter-Saturn, Jupiter-Mercury, and so on.
