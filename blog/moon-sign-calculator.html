What is a Moon Sign (Rasi)?

In Vedic astrology, your Moon sign (called Janma Rasi or simply Rasi) is the zodiac sign the Moon was transiting at the moment of your birth. Unlike Western astrology which emphasises the Sun sign, Vedic astrology considers the Moon sign to be the primary indicator of your personality, mind, and emotional nature.

Your Rasi determines:

  • Your Vimshottari Dasha starting planet and balance at birth
  • Your Nakshatra (birth star — based on Moon's exact degree)
  • Your Sade Sati periods (when Saturn transits over your Rasi)
  • Your daily Rasi Palan (horoscope based on Moon transit)
  • Your Chandrabala — daily strength for auspicious timing

Vedic vs Western Moon Sign

Most online moon sign calculators use the tropical zodiac (Western astrology). The Vedic (sidereal) zodiac uses the Lahiri ayanamsa correction of ~23.85° — shifting all signs back by approximately one sign. This means:

  • If Western says Moon in Taurus → Vedic Rasi is likely Aries (Mesha)
  • If Western says Moon in Scorpio → Vedic Rasi is likely Libra (Thula)
  • The exact shift depends on your birth date (ayanamsa changes slightly each year)

Our calculator uses the Lahiri ayanamsa standard — the same used by the Indian government's official Panchang.
