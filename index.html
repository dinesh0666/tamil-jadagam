Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jathagam?
Jathagam (ஜாதகம்) is the Tamil word for a Vedic birth chart — a map of planetary positions at the moment of your birth, used for astrological analysis.
Is it free?
Yes! Rasi chart, nakshatra, planetary positions, and current dasha are completely free. Premium features (Navamsa, full dasha timeline, Ashtakavarga, PDF) unlock for ₹99 one-time.
How accurate are the calculations?
We use Lahiri (Chitrapaksha) ayanamsa — the standard for Vedic astrology. Planetary positions are accurate to within ~1° for most planets, sufficient for all chart purposes.
I don't know my birth time. What happens?
Check "Time not known" and we'll use 12:00 noon. All planets will still be placed correctly — only the Lagna (Ascendant) and some house-dependent interpretations will be approximate.
Can I share my chart?
Yes! Every chart gets a unique shareable link. You can share on WhatsApp, download as PNG, or save as PDF (premium).
