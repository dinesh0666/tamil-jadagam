Frequently Asked Questions

What is Porutham / Kundali Matching?
Porutham (பொருத்தம்) is Tamil astrology's method of checking marriage compatibility. It compares the Nakshatra (birth star) of the bride and groom across 10 dimensions — Dinam, Ganam, Mahendram, Sthree Dheerga, Yoni, Rasi, Rasi Athipathi, Rajju, Vedha, and Vashyam.
What is a good Guna score?
Out of 36 gunas, a score of 18+ is considered acceptable, 24+ is good, and 30+ is excellent. Rajju and Vedha are considered non-negotiable — a match fails if either is negative.
What is Mangal Dosha?
Mangal Dosha (செவ்வாய் தோஷம்) occurs when Mars is placed in the 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house. Traditionally, two Manglik partners cancel each other's dosha.
